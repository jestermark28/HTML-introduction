• Communication: Effective communication is the ability to convey information clearly and concisely, both verbally and in writing. It involves active listening, empathy, and understanding the audience's needs.
  • Time Management: Time management involves planning and exercising control over how much time to spend on specific activities. It enhances productivity and reduces stress by allowing individuals to prioritize tasks and set achievable goals. Effective time management requires self-discipline and the ability to avoid procrastination.
  • Adaptability:* Adaptability is the ability to adjust to new conditions and embrace change positively. In today's fast-paced world, being adaptable is vital for personal and professional growth. It involves open-mindedness, resilience, and a willingness to learn from experiences.
